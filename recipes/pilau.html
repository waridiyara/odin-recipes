Pilau Recipe.

Pilau is one of the most popular rice dishes in kenya. This tasty coastal dish never misses on the menu at social gatherings and is a regular in many homes.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves crushed
  • A little ginger crushed
  • 2-3 tablespoons ground Pilau masala (depending on one's spice preference).
  • 1/2kg cubed beef
  • 4 tomatoes(finely diced or blended) or 450g tomato paste
  • 2 cups rice
  • 3 cups hot water
  • salt to taste
  • cooking oil as required(2-2.5 tablespoons)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot, and in the onion and ginger. Let this cook over a medium flame until browned.
  2. Stir in the meat and replace lid over pot. Let the meet cook for aout 3 minutes (the meet should be tender). If the meat has not softned allow it to cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Add pilau masala and salt while stirring continuously to ensure the spices are well-distrubuted. (you may also add a little water incase your meat is dry and sticking to the pot.
  4. Add tomato paste and continue to stir. Add some water and bring to a boil after covering the pot. This mixture should get the consistency of a thick sauce.
  5. Add the washed rice and mix well. Proceed to add the 3 cups of water, cover and let this boil over a medium flame for about 5 mins.
  6. Reduce the heat and allow the rice to simmer while covered. After about 5 minutes of cooking,give the rice a stir using a fork and cover.
  7. Repeat the stirring one or two more times to ensure the rice is cooking evenly and does not stick to the bottom of the pot.
  8. the rice should cook until all the excess water has evaporated.
  9. Once ready, the dish may be accompanied with some kachumbari.
